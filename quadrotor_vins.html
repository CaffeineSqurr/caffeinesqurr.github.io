Quadrotor VINS

Recent miniaturization in inertial measurement units (IMU) makes them perfect sensors for environments where GPS isn't present. Additionally IMU's are particularly suited for determining the 3D position of a fast moving vehicle executing dynamic maneuvers.

VINS or vision-aided inertial navigation systems combine an IMU with a camera to reduce the errors induced by the integration of the IMU's bias and noise. We combine a VINS system with a cheap Parrot AR.Drone quad-rotor to demonstrate a real-time implementation on a low cost platform. This demonstration shows that we can precisely locate a fast moving vehicle operating in 3 dimensions without the use of GPS.
